Mount Desert Island

When Michelle & Mike first started dating, they went on a vacation to Mount Desert Island in Maine. Named by a French explorer "Ile de Monts Deserts", literally "island of the bare mountains", it is a panoply of fantastic mountain vistas and enchanting rocky coastlines.

For much of the 19th century Mount Desert Island served as a summer retreat for some of the most prominent people of the time. These included Rockefellers, Morgans, Fords, Vanderbilts, Carnegies, and Astors.

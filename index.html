About Us

Michelle & Mike first met 12 years ago and have been together ever since! They love gardening together, cooking and spending time with their little dog Reggie. Last fall, on Michelle's birthday, Mike got down on one knee and popped the question.

Over the years Michelle & Mike have loved going on trips together up the Maine coast, and Mount Desert Island has always been a special place for them.

About Mount Desert Island

Home to Acadia National Park, Mount Desert Island features spectacular natural scenery. There are many things to do including Hiking & Biking in Acadia, Astronomy & Stargazing, Birdwatching, Photography, Shopping and Restaurants.

Michelle & Mike are registered at Bed, Bath & Beyond.
